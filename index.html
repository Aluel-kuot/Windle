DAFI SCHOLARSHIP ANNOUNCEMENT

UNHCR ALBERT EINSTEIN GERMANY ACADEMIC REFUGEE INITIATIVE

This is a life changing opportunity for young youths
in the camps who have not got a chance to further their education
due to the situation and the challenges they face as refugees
They get a chance of having full scholarship for the courses of their choice.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Must be a registered refugee
  • Must be 18-26 years old
  • Must be residing in a known camp in the countries mentioned
  • Must have a grade of C+ and above

HOW TO APPLY

Application forms can be found at the Windle Trust office in any of the camps.
Applicants must have all the academics documents and their refugee registration
form ,Alien Id or Waiting card
You must scan the above mention in one document and attach them to the form.
